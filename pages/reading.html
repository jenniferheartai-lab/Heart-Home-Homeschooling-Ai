Reading

Reading Focus

Phonics, fluency, narration, comprehension, and a love for good books.

Simple Family Method

Read aloud together, pause for questions, sound out hard words slowly, and let the child retell what they heard.

Weekly Practice

Day 1: New passage. Day 2: Re-read with support. Day 3: Narrate. Day 4: Vocabulary. Day 5: Read with confidence.
