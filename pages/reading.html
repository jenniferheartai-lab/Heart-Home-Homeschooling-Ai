Language Arts

Reading

✦

Build confident readers through phonics, fluency, narration, comprehension, and a steady love for good books.

Start with Kindergarten See 1st Grade

Reading Focus

Phonics, fluency, vocabulary, comprehension, and joyful daily practice.

Simple Family Method

Read aloud together, pause for questions, sound out tricky words slowly, and let the child retell what was heard.

Weekly Practice

Day 1 new passage, Day 2 re-read, Day 3 narration, Day 4 vocabulary, Day 5 confident reading.

Helpful Reminder

Reading grows best in short, calm, repeated practice instead of one giant battle before lunch.

“Little by little, with patience and warmth, a child becomes a reader.”
